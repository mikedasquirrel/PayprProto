Case Study: Smerconish-style Network

What changes when a site like Smerconish.com turns on tiny, instant unlocks? Less second-hand rewrites, more original reporting, and direct support for contributors—without the subscription wall.

No “free rewrite” incentive

Tiny prices reduce the incentive to hunt for free copycat summaries. Readers pay cents for the real thing, not a derivative.

Local + independent uplift

A single wallet travels across local publications and independents. Discovery on the rack funnels real money to smaller newsrooms.

Contributor splits

Define author/editor/publisher splits in Paypr. Each unlock routes the net cents to the right parties automatically.

Calm reader experience

No nag modals, no bundles. Preview, pay once, read. Refunds within 10 minutes keep trust high.

Example split

A 50¢ unlock with 10% platform fee leaves 45¢ net. With 60% author / 20% editor / 20% publisher, payouts land as:

  • Author: 27¢
  • Editor: 9¢
  • Publisher: 9¢
